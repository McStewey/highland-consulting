Highland is a transformation consultancy for leaders who need more than advice. We bring structure to complexity and stay close enough to the work to make change real.

Our point of view

We don’t hand over a deck and disappear.

Organizations are living systems. A change in strategy affects structure. Structure affects workflow. Workflow affects people, technology, customers, and cash. Treating any one of those in isolation creates elegant answers that fail in practice.

We work across the system. We ask hard questions, make the complex visible, build with the people closest to the work, and stay engaged through implementation.

Built with people

The organization already holds more of the answer than most consulting models allow.

Our role is to surface that knowledge, connect it across functions, and turn it into decisions and operating practices the team can own.

Leadership

Experience across the whole enterprise.

FOUNDER + PRINCIPAL

Wade Stewart, MBA

A business transformation operator with three decades of experience spanning organizational design, development finance, human resources, banking, sales, technology adoption, training, and complex project execution.

Wade’s career has repeatedly placed him at the intersection of systems and people: opening and operating financial branches, redesigning agency-wide HR and training infrastructure, structuring capital strategies for builders and developers, implementing business technology, and creating the operating tools teams use to make better decisions.

His work is grounded in a simple belief: the best strategy is the one people can understand, leaders can govern, and the organization can execute. Clients value his ability to see across functions, simplify complexity, and move from conversation to working solution.
